Arrangements

Mehamerchakim- מהמרחקים

An innovative arrangement of the nostalgic Israeli song "Mehamerchakim" by Sagiv Cohen.
features piano, a string quartet, and a female choir, with May Peled as the lead vocalist.
Matan's arrangement adds rich harmonies, new time signature changes, and an exciting modulation. Performed in the Shirimon Contest's Final.
May 2023

Don't Let Me Be Misunderstood

A jazzy and complex arrangement of the old known song by The Animals.
With the sensitive music management and playing of Daniel Ian Smith, Berklee's professor and an amazing Saxophone player and musician.
October 2022

Alone Together- Big Band Arrangement

A unique arrangement from Matan's first experience in writing for big band in Rimon School of Music in Israel.
The jazz standard cover shows advanced concepts of harmony and rhythm, especially in the solo parts.
July 2021

At Last

A special arrangement featuring 3 horns, upright bass, piano, drums and a lead vocal.
This arrangement is breaking boundries with time signature changes between the verses and choruses and extremley interesting and unique voicings for 3 horns arrangement.
