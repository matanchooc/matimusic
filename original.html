Original Songs

Ha'acheret Hazot- האחרת הזאת

A song Matan composed based on the lyrics of Rachel the poet, and added two verses of his own interpretation.
The song was composed during a songwriting class with Yoni rechter, one of Israel's biggest composers, in Rimon School of Music.
April 2023

Ga'aguim- געגועים

A song Matan composed and wrote for a songwriting class in Rimon School of Music with Yehuda Eder, the school's president.
The song speaks about longings to a place where you felt belonged to, and now you feel like your'e living your past by thinking and missing those times.
June 2021
